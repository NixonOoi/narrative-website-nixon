Lady Godiva
Story Theory & Controversy Lessons to Learn Influence on Society

A courageous woman's legendary ride through Coventry to challenge injustice

Posted by Nixon Ooi on November 28, 2023

The tale of Lady Godiva is one that people would call audacious and peculiar. Taking pity on the people of coventry who were suffering from high taxes set by her husband Leofric (Earl of Mercia), Lady Godiva’s appeal for the people was returned with a dare if you would say to strut through the streets of town fully nude. Lady Godiva, passionate as a women she was took the dare head on, stripping off her clothes with only her long hair for cover. Another golden nugget from this story is the origin of “peeping tom”, whom was a man caught in the act of voyeurism as the whole town stayed indoors to provide Lady Godiva the privacy she deserved making the ultimate sacrifice for the people. Tom was eventually given the punishment to become blind for his actions.


Theory & Controversy

Nudity, in particular female nudity, is not commonly weaponised for protest in those days compared to its use for economic and political gains. There are many plot holes regarding the element of nudity itself. The idea for a women to volunteer herself in this manner seemed orthodox, even the request put in by her husband himself likewise seems extreme. Nonetheless, that is how the story is told.

Lady Godiva is reduced to a naked female body which empowers women to defend their rights on the one hand, and becomes a commodity exhibited by brands on the other.

Date of Origin

Est 980 - 1086

Location: Coventry




Lessons to Learn

Selflessness

Godiva's story is one of self-sacrifice and devotion. Understanding her duty to her citizens was more important than her own feelings and desires.

Modesty and Humility

We often overlook the virtue of modesty, as Lady Godiva supposedly rode through Coventry unclothed, symbolizing a humble approach to addressing grievances.

Embracing Change

Earl Leofric's acceptance of Lady Godiva's actions could be seen as a lesson in being open to change in addressing societal concerns.




Influence on society

Kinship

In this era, the origin of kinship system is the mutual exchange of women. On the economic plane, Leofric shows his favor by exchanging his wife’s body for the loyalty of his people.

Understanding the male gaze

Woman is always represented as ‘other’, as an object rather than a subject, materializing man’s unconscious in the films (Sassatelli, 2011).

Godiva Chocolatier brand

Founded in 1926, Godiva Chocolatier places values such as “nobility, courage, freedom” at the core of its brand vision by associating the origin of the chocolates it produces with the story of Lady Godiva.
